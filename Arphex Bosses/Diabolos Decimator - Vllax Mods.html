The Diabolos Decimator is a BOSS entity - the strongest outside of the Tormentor.

Click here for a guide on how to defeat this boss. Summary: Avoid fighting it in the crawling dimension where it spawns naturally, using the summoning structure instead. Ideally, use dual Chrono Cannons, Abyss Ascendant, and prot 4 Juggernaut Armour. Different attacks warrant different approaches - for some like the black hole power and charge attacks, you'll want to stay as far away as possible, while for others like the genesis rifle bursts, they can be easier to dodge at close range before they start homing in on the target.

Overview

The Diabolos Decimator is a huge, extremely dangerous boss. The second of the two crawling dimension demonic stalker bosses, it manifests as a hybrid of a giant centipede and "diabolical ironclad beetle" with golden scarab elements. The Decimator name matches up with the Trisector (the other crawling dimension boss). This boss is so strong that it can even significantly harm the tier 1 Tormentor final boss in combat.

It appears in multiple versions. It can appear as an original version or an enormous shadow clone. The main version can stalk towards its player target and pose a major threat, even with strong boss gear. Also summonable by placing a scorched sand block on top of a Heavy Chitin Block, then lighting it on fire with a scorch charge.

On death; it drops the highly valuable Entropy Matrix.

Abilities

The abilities of the Diabolos Decimator include:

  • Full stalking mechanism where it can follow you across the crawling, tracking the player across long distances. Most of its attacks are airborne - it prioritises flight for movement.
  • Shuffled attacks (all attacks cycle through each time in a random order) with primarily cosmic powers:
  • Mode 1: Oblivion attacks - Attacks with lasers similar to the oblivion ray weapon, attempting to melt down its opponent - sometimes summons two clones during this phase that may also fire lasers at the target.
  • Mode 2: Self heal and homing attacks. Summons “Entropy Conduits” that float midair and heal the Diabolos when nearby, unless destroyed quickly (provides some health to the entity that destroys them and damages the Diabolos if they are destroyed). These conduits follow the diabolos if it floats up or down, and can be destroyed in a single hit by a dual wield Chrono Cannon shot. During this mode it also fires dark homing “graviton” projectiles, especially if low on health.
  • Mode 3: Deadly charge attack - charges in an extremely accurate+deadly way towards its target, destroying any barriers with explosions. Different charge animations depending on whether it is airborne or near the ground.
  • Mode 4: Golden cosmic power+galaxy mode - the Diabolos summons a galaxy around itself that provides Supergravity effects on contact, plus summons an array of deadly beams from the sky that fall down, forcing the target to dodge. It also becomes invincible and approaches the target in this mode, along with dealing heavy damage to any mobs that get too close.
  • Mode 5: Monstrous growth and black hole ability - grows to the size of a mountain (can vary depending on what mob it is fighting), becoming invincible and summoning a black hole that sucks in nearby entities, killing them instantly on contact. You do not want to use pets against this boss, as they may be obliterated by such an attack - escapable by flying away or teleporting before it can pull you in. Below half health, it can also fire graviton projectiles in this mode.
  • Mode 6: Genesis rifle shots - fires repeated clusters of genesis rifle blasts with a bonus periodic ability to change direction towards the target midair, if they haven’t already gone past.
  • Passive abilities: Forcefield and weak projectile repulsion, plus time-based damage limits (if it takes damage unexpectedly fast, it will cancel that damage).
  • Heavy melee damage, best not to get close

Other details

Other notes about the Diabolos Decimator:

  • Note that when it kills a player, it will despawn, unless the player it killed had a bane of the darkness in their inventory. This prevents repeated spawnkilling and limits the amount that the diabolos decimator can be used as a potential weapon against other players via summoning.
  • Hallucinations/jumpscares flashing on the screen
  • Reaction to being looked at
  • Credit to Moclifftobju for major beneficial suggestions around this original boss concept!
  • Eerie clones appear nearby to distract the player, occasionally teleporting closer. Variable sizes including enormous giants can spawn.
  • All attack phases are contained within a single entity, rather than spawning multiple versions
  • Turns a dark black shade when below half health

Lore concept:

This demon lord is an overwhelmingly powerful, intelligent, and ancient deity, with its existence preceding even that of the Tormentor. It was powerful enough to survive the cataclysmic absorption event that led to the Tormentor's existence, and retains its position as the strongest demon lord outside of that omnipotent being. The Diabolos possesses a wide range of cosmic powers, capable of bringing forth compressed versions of celestial bodies for use as attacks, calling them forth from other dimensions, in miniaturised forms. Almost nothing can stand up to such firepower, leading to this entity ruling over vast sectors of the crawling dimension. Its centipede-beetle morphology provides it with maximum mobility, shielding, and attack potency, even outside of its cosmic powers. Typically, combining wings with so many legs would be impractical and unsustainable for arthropods, but with such a level of demonic scorch power, the Diabolos easily compensates for any additional energy requirements. Entropy is another defining characteristic of the Diabolos' power, making it an agent of chaos - it can reduce its opponents into cosmic dust, exploiting matter itself as a weakness. Regular defences are rendered useless against the acceleration of such a cosmically fundamental and inevitable force.
